**8. In a class, 15% of students fail in Science, 25% in Maths, and 10% in both. How much % of students passed in both subjects?**

**Ans:**
$$n(A \cup B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A \cap B)$$
$$= 15 + 25 - 10 = 30\%$$
Since 30% of students are failing in at least one subject, thus:
$$100 - 30 = 70\%$$
70% of students are passing the exams.
